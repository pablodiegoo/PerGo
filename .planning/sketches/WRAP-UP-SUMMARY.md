## Design Direction
A lightweight, clean, and essentially monochromatic Notion-inspired user interface for developers/makers and operators. Focuses on clarity, high typography readability (Inter), and functional colors (green/blue for status/events) over decorative elements. Includes dynamic layout switching to guide first-time users before presenting complex dashboards.

## Key Decisions
- **Layout & Navigation:** Notion-style collapsible wide sidebar (Variant A) won due to its superior readability and accessibility of hubs.
- **Onboarding Flow:** Dynamic onboarding wizard checklist (Variant A) guides first-time users through keys, connections, testing, and webhooks.
- **Operational Interface:** Returning user console (Variant B) prioritizes multi-instance connections, telemetry metrics, and a live audit log simulator.
- **Theme:** Default monochromatic grayscale theme with sharp typography, thin borders, and subtle shadow cards.
