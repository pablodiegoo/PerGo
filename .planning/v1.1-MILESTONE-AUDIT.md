## Tech Debt & Warnings

### ⚠️ Sidebar Active Highlighting Mapping Bug (WARNING)
* **Description**: Visiting the Campaigns page redirects the user from `/admin/campaigns` to a workspace-scoped path: `/admin/workspaces/:workspace_id/campaigns`.
* **Root Cause**:
  1. The `isTopLevelActive` helper in `sidebar.templ` only checks if the path begins with `/admin/campaigns`, returning `false` for the redirected workspace-scoped path.
  2. The `isSettingsActive` helper matches `/admin/workspaces`, which expands the settings accordion and highlights the "Workspace" sub-option incorrectly.
* **Resolution**: Adjust `isSettingsActive` and `isSubmenuActive` to exclude campaign paths, and modify `isTopLevelActive` to match workspace-scoped campaign URLs.
